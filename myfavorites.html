My Favorites

Here are some of my favorite things that make me happy and inspire me.

Chess

I often space out, and I don't like it. Chess allows me to be able to pour all of my focus in calculation, which frees me. But it is stressful.

League of Legends

League of Legends is very engaging, and requires you to play as carefully as possible to gain/keep an advantage which you can lose at any given time (and do so with a team). But it is even more stressful.

YouTube

I watch YouTube videos to get rid of the stress. Before repeating the cycle.
